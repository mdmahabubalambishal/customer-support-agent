## App Issues

Download our app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
If our app is crashing, update to the latest version first.
Uninstall and reinstall our app if crashes continue after update.
Our app requires Android 8.0 or iOS 13 and above.
Contact support if our app issues persist after reinstallation.

## OTP Not Received

OTP is sent within 60 seconds via SMS and email.
Check spam or junk folder for email OTP.
Ensure mobile number has signal and is not on DND.
Request OTP again after 2 minutes if not received.
If mobile OTP is not working, try email OTP option.
Contact support if issue continues for more than 10 minutes.

Keywords: OTP aseni, OTP problem, verification code, OTP not received,

## Cart and Checkout Issues

If items are not adding to cart on our website, refresh the page and try again.
Cart items may be removed if a product goes out of stock.
If our checkout page has issues, try a different browser.
Payment page error on our site: try a different payment method.
Do not press back button during payment processing on our website.

## Search and Filter Issues

If search on our website is not showing results, check for spelling errors.
Use general keywords instead of very specific phrases for better results.
Filter options reset after leaving the page, reapply filters as needed.
Newly added products may take up to 2 hours to appear in search results.

Keywords: search kaj korche na, product khuje pacchi na, filter problem,

## Notification Issues

Enable notifications in our app settings to receive order updates.
SMS notifications are sent to your registered phone number automatically.
Email notifications may go to spam, add our email to your contacts.
To change notification preferences go to My Account then Notification Settings.
Push notifications require app permission for our app.
