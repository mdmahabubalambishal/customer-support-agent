## Shipping Options

We offer two shipping options: Standard and Express delivery.
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and costs 60 BDT.
Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs 120 BDT.
Free standard shipping is available on orders above 1000 BDT.
Same-day delivery is available in Dhaka for orders placed before 11 AM.
Same-day delivery costs 150 BDT and is limited to selected areas.

## Delivery Areas

We deliver to all 64 districts of Bangladesh.
Dhaka city delivery takes 1-3 business days.
Outside Dhaka delivery takes 3-7 business days.
Remote areas may take up to 10 business days.
Check if your area is serviceable by entering your postal code on the website.
International shipping is currently not available.

## Shipping and Dispatch

Orders are processed and dispatched within 24 hours of confirmation.
Dispatch confirmation with tracking number is sent via SMS and email.
Orders placed on Friday after 5 PM are dispatched on Sunday.
Public holidays may cause 1-2 day delays in dispatch.
Bulk orders above 10 items may take 48 hours to dispatch.

## Courier Partners

We use Pathao, Paperfly, Sundarban, and RedX as our courier partners.

You can see your assigned courier in the tracking page after dispatch.
For courier-specific complaints, we will handle them on your behalf.
Do not contact courier directly for complaints — always contact our support.

## Delivery Attempt and Failed Delivery

Our courier will attempt delivery up to 3 times.
If not available, the courier will call before the next attempt.
After 3 failed attempts, the order returns to our warehouse.
Returned orders will be reshipped upon customer request within 7 days.
Additional shipping charges may apply for reshipping.
Ensure your phone number and address are correct when placing the order.

## Damaged During Shipping

If product is damaged during shipping, report within 24 hours of delivery.
Take photos of the damaged packaging and product before opening fully.
Send photos to support@company.com with your Order ID.
We will arrange free return pickup and send a replacement within 3-5 days.
Full refund is available if replacement is not preferred.
Damaged items should not be used or disposed of before resolution.
