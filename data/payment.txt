## Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express.
Debit cards from all major banks in Bangladesh.
Mobile banking: bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and Upay.
Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for orders within Bangladesh.
Bank transfer is available for orders above 5000 BDT.
All online payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

## Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery is available for all areas in Bangladesh.
COD orders require exact change as couriers may not carry change.
COD is not available for orders above 10,000 BDT.
COD orders take slightly longer to process than prepaid orders.
Repeated COD order cancellations may result in COD being disabled for your account.

## Payment Failed

If your payment failed, do not attempt the transaction again immediately.
Wait 10 minutes and check your bank account or mobile banking for deduction.
If money was deducted but order not confirmed, contact support with transaction ID.
Failed payment refunds are processed within 5-7 business days automatically.
Common reasons for payment failure: wrong OTP, insufficient balance, bank server issues.
Try a different payment method if the issue persists.

## Refund Policy

Refunds are issued for: cancelled orders, returned items, and payment errors.
Refund timeline depends on payment method:
  - bKash, Nagad, Rocket: 1-3 business days
  - Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  - Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days
  - COD orders: refunded via bKash or bank transfer within 5 days.
Refund confirmation is sent via email and SMS.
If refund not received within timeline, contact support with Order ID.

## Invoice and Receipt

Digital invoice is automatically sent to your registered email after purchase.
Invoice can also be downloaded from "My Orders" section in your account.
For VAT invoice requests, contact support with your Order ID and company details.
Physical invoice is included inside the delivery package.
Invoice re-sending can be requested by contacting support.

## EMI Options

EMI is available on orders above 3000 BDT with selected credit cards.
Supported banks for EMI: BRAC Bank, Dutch Bangla, City Bank, Standard Chartered.
EMI tenures available: 3, 6, and 12 months.
Zero interest EMI is available on selected products during promotional periods.
EMI option appears at checkout if your card is eligible.
Contact your bank directly for EMI-related issues after order placement.

## Discount Codes and Vouchers

Discount codes can be applied at checkout in the "Promo Code" field.
Each promo code can be used once per account unless stated otherwise.
Promo codes are case-sensitive — enter exactly as provided.
Codes cannot be applied after order placement.
If a valid code is not working, contact support with the code and screenshot.
Promo codes cannot be combined with other offers unless specified.
