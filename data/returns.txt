## Return Policy Overview

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products from the date of delivery.
Electronics and gadgets have a 3-day return window.
Clothing and footwear have a 7-day return window.
Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached.
Sale items and undergarments are non-returnable unless defective.
Customized or personalized products cannot be returned.

## How to Initiate a Return

To return an item, follow these steps:
Step 1: Go to "My Orders" in your account and click "Return Request".
Step 2: Select the item and reason for return.
Step 3: Upload photos of the item and packaging.
Step 4: Submit the return request.
Our team will review and approve within 24 hours.
After approval, a courier will pick up the item from your address within 2 days.
Return pickup is completely free of charge.

## Return Pickup

After return approval, courier will contact you to schedule pickup.
Pickup is available Monday to Saturday between 10 AM and 6 PM.
Keep the item packed in original box with all accessories and tags.
If original box is unavailable, use any secure packaging.
Return pickup is free — do not pay any amount to the courier.
If courier does not arrive within 3 days of approval, contact support.

## Exchange Policy

Exchange is available for size or color variation of the same product.
Exchange request must be made within 7 days of delivery.
The replacement item will be sent after we receive the returned item.
If requested size or color is unavailable, a full refund will be issued.
Exchange is free of charge for the first request per order.
Products must be in original condition for exchange to be processed.

## Defective Product Return

Defective products can be returned or exchanged within 30 days of delivery.
Defects include: manufacturing faults, broken parts, non-functional items.
Report defect with clear photos or video showing the issue.
Send evidence to support@company.com with your Order ID.
Free return pickup and priority replacement within 2-3 business days.
If replacement is unavailable, full refund is processed within 3-5 days.

## Refund After Return

Refund is processed after we receive and inspect the returned item.
Inspection takes 1-2 business days after receiving the return.
Refund timeline after inspection:
  - bKash, Nagad, Rocket: 1-3 business days
  - Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  - COD orders: bKash or bank transfer within 5 days
Refund confirmation is sent via email and SMS.
Original shipping charge is non-refundable unless item was defective.
