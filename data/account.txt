## Account Registration

Create an account by clicking "Sign Up" on our website or app.
Registration requires a valid email address and Bangladeshi phone number.
OTP verification is required for both email and phone during registration.
One account per person is allowed — multiple accounts may be suspended.
Registration is free and takes less than 2 minutes.
You can also register using your Google or Facebook account.

## Login Issues

If you cannot log in, first try resetting your password.
Make sure you are using the email or phone number used during registration.
Try using a different browser or incognito mode if login fails.
If account is locked after multiple failed attempts, wait 30 minutes before retrying.
For persistent login issues, contact support with your registered email or phone.
Avoid logging in from multiple devices simultaneously.

Keywords: login hocche na, লগইন সমস্যা, cant login, password forgot, account access, sign in problem

## Password Reset

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
Enter your registered email address or phone number.
OTP will be sent to your email and phone within 2 minutes.
Enter the OTP and set a new password.
New password must be at least 8 characters with one number and one letter.
Password reset link expires after 15 minutes.
If OTP not received, check spam folder or request again after 2 minutes.

Keywords: password reset, পাসওয়ার্ড ভুলে গেছি, password change, forgot password, নতুন পাসওয়ার্ড, OTP aseni

## Profile Update

Update your profile by going to "My Account" and clicking "Edit Profile".
You can update: name, email, phone number, and profile picture.
Phone number change requires OTP verification on new number.
Email change requires verification on new email address.
Changes are saved immediately after verification.

Keywords: profile update, name change, phone number change, email change, account edit, তথ্য পরিবর্তন

## Delivery Address Management

Save multiple delivery addresses in your account for faster checkout.
Go to "My Account" and click "Manage Addresses" to add or edit addresses.
You can set a default address for quick ordering.
Address can also be changed during checkout before order confirmation.
Up to 5 addresses can be saved per account.

Keywords: address add, ঠিকানা পরিবর্তন, delivery address, new address, address save, address update

## Account Security

We recommend enabling two-factor authentication for account security.
Never share your OTP or password with anyone including our support team.
Our support team will never ask for your password.
If you suspect unauthorized access, change password immediately and contact support.
Suspicious login alerts are sent via email and SMS automatically.

Keywords: account security, হ্যাক হয়েছে, unauthorized access, account safe, two factor, account compromised

---

## Account Deletion

To delete your account, go to "My Account" and click "Delete Account".
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
All order history and personal data will be removed within 30 days.
Pending orders must be completed or cancelled before account deletion.
Unused wallet balance must be withdrawn before deletion.
Contact support if you want to temporarily deactivate instead of delete.

Keywords: account delete, account বন্ধ করতে চাই, account deactivate, remove account, delete my account
