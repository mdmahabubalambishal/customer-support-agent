## Order Placement

Customers can place orders by adding items to the cart and proceeding to checkout.
You need a registered account or can checkout as a guest.
After placing an order, a confirmation email and SMS will be sent within 5 minutes.
Each order receives a unique Order ID which can be used for tracking and support.
Orders are accepted 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
Minimum order value is 200 BDT for standard delivery.

## Order Confirmation

After placing an order, you will receive a confirmation email and SMS.
If you do not receive confirmation within 10 minutes, check your spam folder.
Order confirmation contains your Order ID, item list, and estimated delivery date.
If payment was deducted but no confirmation received, contact support immediately.
Confirmation can also be viewed in your account under "My Orders" section.

## Order Tracking

Track your order by visiting our website and going to "My Orders" section.
Enter your Order ID and registered phone number on the tracking page.
A tracking number from our courier partner is sent via SMS after dispatch.
Tracking updates are refreshed every 12 hours.
If tracking shows "Out for Delivery" but not received by end of day, contact support.
If tracking shows "Delivered" but item not received, report within 48 hours.

## Order Modification

Orders can be modified within 2 hours of placement if not yet processed.
Modifications include: changing size, color, quantity, or delivery address.
To modify, contact support immediately with your Order ID and required changes.
Once an order is packed or shipped, modifications are not possible.
Adding new items to an existing order is not supported; place a new order instead.

Keywords: order change korte chai, size change, address change, quantity change, অর্ডার পরিবর্তন, modify order, order edit

## Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement if not yet shipped.
To cancel, go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order" or contact support.
Once an order is shipped, it cannot be cancelled — you must request a return instead.
Cancelled order refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
For prepaid orders, refund goes back to original payment method.
Cash on delivery orders have no cancellation charge.

Keywords: order cancel korte chai, cancel order, অর্ডার বাতিল করতে চাই, cancel hobe ki, order cancel, refund after cancel

## Wrong or Missing Item

If you received a wrong item, report within 48 hours of delivery with photo proof.
Missing items from an order must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.
Send photos of the received item and packaging to support@company.com.
We will arrange a free return pickup and send the correct item within 3-5 days.
Full refund is also available if replacement is not preferred.

## Duplicate Order

If a duplicate order was placed accidentally, contact support immediately.
Duplicate orders can be cancelled free of charge within 24 hours.
If payment was charged twice, refund for the duplicate will be processed in 3-5 days.
To avoid duplicates, do not click the order button multiple times.

Keywords: double order, duplicate order, same order twice, একই অর্ডার দুইবার, accidental order, extra order cancel
